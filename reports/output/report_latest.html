Factors Affecting Access to External Financing (Q11)

  • A net +6.4% of Slovak firms (n=270) reported improved willingness of banks to provide credit in Wave 38, broadly in line with the EA average of +5.6%, though easing from a net +8.3% in the previous wave.
  • A net -44.1% of Slovak firms (n=398) reported a deteriorating general economic outlook, a sharper decline than the EA average of -26.9% and worsening from a net -37.7% in Wave 37, signalling a growing macro-level drag.
  • Data on access to public financial support was unavailable for Slovakia in Wave 38 (n/a), preventing comparison with the EA net balance of -16.6% or Slovakia's own prior reading of -28.5% in Wave 37.
